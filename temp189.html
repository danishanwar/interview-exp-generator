We are given 2 long ints as inputs and we return a vector which is path between them.

I discussed with him to go from \xe2\x80\x9cnode to root\xe2\x80\x9d instead of general \xe2\x80\x9croot to node\xe2\x80\x9d. He asked me how and I found out LCA using map and stack.
