  2. A kind of design question. Given amazon log files of two days.He asked me to find the users who logged in both the days. Had discussion using Hashmap,he want to optimise further I gave solution using trie with little modification in its structure\xc2\xa0
    \xc2\xa0
struct Trie {\n    int count[2],\n    bool isleaf;\n    Trie *child[26];\n    Trie() {\n          count[0]=count[1]=0;\n          isleaf = false;\n          for(int i=0;i < 26;i++)\n          child[i]=NULL;\n    }\n};
