Recently Microsoft visited our campus for SDE intern. The process took over 4 months.

Round 1 (Online MCQ\xe2\x80\x99s)
Time-30 mins
This round consisted of 25 questions on C,C++,Aptitude(Probability) and OS.
60 students out of 150 were shortlisted for the next round.

Time-90mins
1) Write a function to check whether the string is valid or not.(Check Balanced Parenthesis{},[],()).
*/header Stack.h was added in C++/*
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/check-for-balanced-parentheses-in-an-expression/

2) Write a recursive function to form a list containing intersection of two given linked list without creating new nodes.

37 students were shorlisted for the next round.
