First technical round:\xc2\xa0

1. Tell me something about yourself.\xc2\xa0
2. Tell me something not on your resume.\xc2\xa0
3. Given an array of integers.If the number a and its negation -a both present in the array then print it.\xc2\xa0

4. LRU implementation\xc2\xa0
I told him an approach having time complexity of O(size of frame).He was not happy with my approach.\xc2\xa0

1. Some SQL queries (related to nested query) and sharding\xc2\xa0

4.Which graph traversal method is better and why.\xc2\xa0
