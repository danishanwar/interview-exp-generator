Online coding round:

It was very easy and there were 20+ MCQs and 2 coding questions-
1.you have a string and you have to replace every character with the character 3 place ahead(like a->d, z ->c, \xe2\x80\xa6.)
2.related to array, I don\xe2\x80\x99t remember it but it was very easy to implement.

2. Tell me something not on your resume.
